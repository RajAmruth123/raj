About the Olympiad

The Rajamruth National Yoga Olympiad is a golden opportunity for students to immerse themselves in the timeless wisdom of ancient India. Through this unique written examination, participants connect deeply with sacred texts like the Bhagavad Gita, Upanishads, and Darshanas, gaining insights into yogic philosophy and mental wellness. This Olympiad not only fosters intellectual growth but also inspires students to apply ancient teachings in real life, promoting inner peace, clarity, and a meaningful journey toward self-realization.

Benefits of Written Olympiad for Mental Health

  • Enhances focus and concentration
  • Encourages reflective thinking and self-awareness
  • Reduces stress through structured mental engagement
  • Promotes a deeper understanding of yogic principles
